What I've Built

Projects

01
Agricultural Robot for Planting & Crop Monitoring
Designed and developed a semi-autonomous robot for seed planting and real-time crop disease detection. Built the full stainless steel chassis in SolidWorks (500 × 300 × 100 mm frame), integrated servo-driven seed dispensing mechanisms, and set up an ESP32-CAM module with a leaf disease detection model that flags issues like blight in real time. Field-tested among live tomato plants. Documented complete BOM and assembly drawings.
  • Designed a stainless steel chassis in SolidWorks (500 × 300 × 100 mm) optimised for field terrain and component access.
  • Built a servo-driven seed dispensing mechanism with adjustable seed spacing.
  • Integrated an ESP32-CAM module running a leaf disease detection model — successfully flagged blight with 0.793 confidence during field testing.
  • Field-tested the robot among live tomato plants to validate mobility and sensor performance under real conditions.
  • Produced full BOM, assembly drawings, and wiring documentation for the build.
